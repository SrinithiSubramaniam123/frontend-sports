Our Facilities

Everything an athlete needs, on one campus.

Training, recovery, nutrition and accommodation are all built into the same six-acre campus — so athletes spend less time travelling and more time training.

🏟️

Multi-Sport Stadium

A full-size floodlit stadium with a synthetic athletics track surrounding the football and field-events area, used for matches, meets and major academy events.

Capacity: 2,500
🏋️

Strength & Conditioning Gym

A fully equipped gym for strength, power and injury-prevention work, programmed individually by sport and training phase under coach supervision.

Open 5:00 AM – 9:00 PM
🏠

Athlete Hostel

On-campus residential blocks for outstation athletes, with shared study areas, dining hall and round-the-clock warden supervision.

120 Beds
🩺

Medical Support

An on-site physiotherapy and first-response medical room staffed during all training hours, with tie-ups to a partner hospital for further care.

On-site Physiotherapist
🎥

Video Analysis Room

High-speed cameras and playback software used to break down technique frame-by-frame — from a bowling action to a swimming turn.

Used Every Session
🥗

Nutrition Guidance

A staff nutritionist builds meal plans around training load and competition calendars, with the academy mess following the same guidelines.

Personalised Meal Plans
🎒

Equipment Provided

Sport-specific equipment is available for training so athletes can start without buying everything on day one.

Structured Training Sessions

Every batch follows a fixed weekly schedule split across technique, fitness and match-play blocks, so progress can be tracked term over term.
